About

We intend to create value and empower all type of businesses

Enterprise Resource Planning System, a software which made a significant impact in the life of Indian Manufacturers by remodeling their existing manual system of working to Automating it.

A system which was ideally designed to track the activities has travelled leaps and miles from being a mere tracking system to a virtual member that monitors, tracks and guides the users in order to streamline and maintain the operational efficiency of the business.

Ever Since 2001, the market has been penetrated with hundreds of ERP players, everyone stating themselves as the best. As we dug down more and more we realized that most of the small & mid size companies still resisting to move towards IT due to multiple reason such as budget constraints, hassle of using 2 different softwares, Security issues, to name a few.

This is how Eazy came into picture with a vision of making latest technology available at affordable prices with its unique preposition and simultaneously catering the key challenges of SME Manufacturers thereby making their lives Easy!

This led to the launch of India’s 1st Tally Integrated ERP, Eazy ERP in 2007.

An ERP with the simplicity of Tally and Power of ERP

The Only Enterprise level ERP bridging the gap between Indian SMEs & Technology by providing an ERP which does not have a finance module & uses their Tally instead, allowing them to easily adapt to new systems without making any changes in Finance sphere.

At Present, Eazy has helped 100’s of companies to manage complete end-to-end manufacturing operations like Sales, Purchase, Stage wise Production Management including WIP, Inventory Management, Quality Control etc. with comprehensive integration with Tally Accounting Software

With time, to cater more and more Companies Eazy ERP made its integration with second widely used accounting software, “Busy”! Also, keeping in mind that companies vary in size and numbers Eazy launched its hybrid model i.e. On-Premises and Saas so that most of small and mid-sized companies can take leverage of it.

Earlier books were known as your buddy. However 21st generation would state mobile gadgets and internet as your Nxt Buddy. Eazy aims to make Eazy ERP 4.0 every manufactures Best Buddy.
