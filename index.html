How it Started

Enterprise Resource Planning System, a software which made a significant impact in the life of Indian Manufacturers by remodeling their existing manual system of working to Automating it.

Ever Since 2001,
    Market has penetrated with hundreds of ERP players, everyone stating themselves as the best.
    Small & mid size companies still resist to move towards IT
    Due to budget constraints, hassle of using 2 different software, Security issues, to name a few.

This led to the launch of India’s 1st Tally Integrated ERP, Eazy ERP in 2007.

With a vision of making latest technology available at affordable prices with its unique proposition and simultaneously catering the key challenges of SME Manufacturers thereby making their lives easy!

An ERP with the simplicity of Tally and Power of ERP

The Only Enterprise level ERP bridging the gap between Indian SMEs & Technology by providing an ERP which does not have a finance module & uses their Tally instead.

Eazy ERP made its integration with second widely used accounting software, “Busy”!

Also, keeping in mind that companies vary in size and numbers Eazy launched its hybrid model
    i.e. On-Premises and Saas

Earlier books were known as your buddy. However 21st generation would state mobile gadgets and internet as your Next Buddy. Eazy aims to make Eazy ERP 4.0 every manufactures Best Buddy.

At Present, Eazy has helped 100’s of companies to manage complete end-to-end manufacturing operations like Sales, Purchase, Stage wise Production Management including WIP, Inventory Management, Quality Control etc. with comprehensive integration with Tally Accounting Software.
